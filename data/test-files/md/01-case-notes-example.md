(35 mph confirmed by EDR download). Blake entered against a steady red signal
per the police report (Officer Reyes, badge #8841) and was cited under
**CVC §21453(a)**. Witness Maria Chen (independent, on foot at the SW corner)
corroborates the red-signal observation. Blake admitted at scene that he was
"running late for a delivery" — captured on dispatch audio at 00:00:12.

Recommended fault split: **85% Blake / 15% Rivera** (small reduction for
potential comparative fault on Rivera's exact pre-impact speed, which the EDR
puts at 38 mph — 3 mph over the limit, but not the proximate cause).

Expected recovery: **$35,700** (85% × $42,000).

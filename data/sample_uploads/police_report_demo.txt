CALIFORNIA TRAFFIC COLLISION REPORT
Report Number: SF-2026-04417
Investigating Agency: San Francisco Police Department, Traffic Division
Date of Collision: March 14, 2026          Time: 08:42 hours
Location: Intersection of Folsom Street and 7th Street, San Francisco, CA

PARTIES
Party 1 (Insured / our claimant): Maria Rivera, operating a 2023 Toyota Camry,
  license 8XYZ123, traveling northbound on 7th Street.
Party 2 (Other driver): Daniel Blake, operating a 2021 Ford F-150,
  license 6ABC789, traveling eastbound on Folsom Street.

NARRATIVE
At approximately 08:42, Party 1 (Rivera) entered the intersection of 7th Street
and Folsom Street while facing a steady green signal in the northbound direction.
Party 2 (Blake) entered the same intersection traveling eastbound against a steady
red signal and struck the front passenger side of Party 1's vehicle.

Independent witness Karen Osei, standing at the northwest corner, stated: "The
silver car had the green. The pickup blew through the red, going maybe 50." The
posted speed limit on Folsom Street at this location is 35 mph. Signal timing
records obtained from the SFMTA confirm the northbound 7th Street approach held a
green indication at the time of impact.

CITATION
Party 2 (Daniel Blake) was cited at the scene under California Vehicle Code
section 21453(a) for failing to stop at a steady red signal. No citation was
issued to Party 1.

VEHICLE DAMAGE
Party 1 (Toyota Camry): heavy damage to the front passenger side door, quarter
panel, and wheel assembly; airbags deployed. Vehicle towed from scene.
Party 2 (Ford F-150): moderate front-end damage to the bumper and grille.

INJURIES
Party 1 reported neck and shoulder pain and was evaluated by paramedics at the
scene. No injuries reported by Party 2.

OFFICER ASSESSMENT
Based on the physical evidence, the witness statement, the signal timing records,
and the citation issued, the primary cause of this collision was Party 2's entry
into the intersection against a red signal in violation of CVC 21453(a).
